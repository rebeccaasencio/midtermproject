Big Plants is a video game concept I created in 2020

Concept Art

Story

Hidden among the grass, the land of Plantopia is home to plants all over the ground. All is well until an evil poisonous mud monster, MUDDOOM, takes over. Spreading poison all across the land, MUDDOOM slowly takes control. In hopes to end this reign of terror, brace leaf warrior Finn and trusted ally Pea Pod go on a quest to defeat MUDDOOM and bring peace back to Plantopia.

Inspiration

Like many people during quarantine, I turned to video games as an escape. While playing, I took a liking to the character designs, colors, and story. I became interested in game development, one day dreaming of making my own video game. I started to create concept art for a plant based adventure game called BIG PLANTS inspired by video games such as, Pikmin, The Legend of Zelda franchise, Stardew Valley, and Paper Mario 64.

Meet the Characters

Finn Fael comes from the fallen leaves of the great wisdom tree. Filled with bravery, compassion, and determination, Finn leads with courage. With his blue fire sword, he can cut through anything.

Pea Pod comes from the burrow grounds of the west. With his ability to cast healing spells, Pea Pod is the healer of the duo.

Muddoom comes from the poisonous fireous depths of the underground. Fire spirited, easy to anger, yet a bit self conscious, Muddoom’s destiny is to take over the grounds with poisonous mud.

Hints

  • To effectively increase the most damage, use Finn's fire sword to attack when Muddoom turns blue
  • Train Pea Pod experience level to gain the ability to learn higher healing spells
  • Spare local enemies to gain more allies
